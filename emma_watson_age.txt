Emma Watson was born on April 15, 1990, making her 33 years old. She is an English actress, model, and activist known for her roles in both blockbusters and independent films, as well as her women's rights work. Watson has received various accolades for her work, including a Young Artist Award and three MTV Movie Awards. She has siblings named Alex Watson, Nina Watson, Lucy Watson, and Toby Watson, and she attended Brown University from 2009 to 2014. Some of her notable movies and TV shows include Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, Beauty and the Beast, and Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows.
